ABOUT US

OUR PROFILE

M.A.N.S. is a company based in Malaysia specializing in automobile care and maintenance services.We provide services for a wide range of automobile. Currently, we have 5 service centres operating within the Subang area.We aim to ensure customer satisfaction and exceed their expectations in terms of quality and value of our service

Our service centres are designed to provide the best environment for satisfying our customers' automotive needs.This is to ensure the comfort of our customers while our team of experts service their cars or other automobiles.The comfortable experience customers get from M.A.N.S. service centres is sure to leave a lasting positive impression on customers.

Our centres are also equipped with state-of-the-art equipment to cater to all of our customers servicing needs. Our experts are also well-trained and areprepared to give advice andd guidance to customers.

OUR PURPOSE

To provide the best automotive servicing possible and provide an enjoyable experience for all customers.


OUR VISION

At M.A.N.S, we have a vision that one day the international community shall recognize Malaysia as a leading country in the world in terms of car service and maintenance. At M.A.N.S, envision us to be the catalyst for innovative changes and approaches within the local motoring industry in the effort to elevate the standard of products and services to a level that receives local as well as international recognition and respect.


OUR MISSION

Our mission at M.A.N.S is to become the leading provider of innovative and integrated automobile services in Malaysia. We plan to position ourselves as an automobile services provider with a brand synonymous with high quality comprehensive services.
